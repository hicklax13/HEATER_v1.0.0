Heater Analytics LLC ("Heater Analytics," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. By accessing or using the Service, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Account Information
When you register for an account, we collect:
- **Email address** — used for account authentication, service communications, and support.
- **Display name or username** — used to personalize your experience within the Service.
- **Password** — stored in hashed, salted form; we never store or transmit plaintext passwords.

### 1.2 Payment Information
Subscription payments are processed by **Stripe, Inc.** When you subscribe, Stripe collects your payment details (credit/debit card number, expiration date, CVV, and billing address) directly and securely. Heater Analytics receives only:
- A tokenized payment method identifier from Stripe.
- Subscription status and billing history (plan tier, renewal date, payment success/failure).

**We do not store, process, or have access to your full credit card number, CVV, or other sensitive payment credentials.** Stripe's privacy practices are described at stripe.com/privacy.

### 1.3 Fantasy League Data (via Third-Party OAuth)
If you connect a fantasy sports platform (such as Yahoo Sports or ESPN) to the Service, you authorize us to retrieve data from that platform on your behalf using OAuth. This may include:
- Your fantasy team roster, lineup, and player assignments.
- Your league settings, scoring categories, and schedule.
- League standings and opponent roster data (to the extent accessible through the platform's API).
- Transaction history (adds, drops, trades) within your league.

We retrieve only the data necessary to provide the Service. We do not access your third-party platform account for any purpose beyond what you have authorized.

### 1.4 Usage Data
We collect anonymized usage data to understand how the Service is used and to improve its features. This includes:
- Pages visited and features accessed within the application.
- Session duration, general geographic region (country/state level), and device/browser type.
- Feature interaction counts (e.g., number of trade analyses run, lineup optimizations performed).
- Error and crash reports.

This usage data is collected in anonymized or pseudonymized form and is not used to identify you individually. See Section 4 for the tools we use.

### 1.5 Communications
If you contact our support team, we collect the content of your communications, your email address, and any other information you choose to provide, for the purpose of responding to and resolving your inquiry.

## 2. How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

### 2.1 Providing and Improving the Service
- To create and manage your account and authenticate your identity.
- To deliver the features and analytical outputs you request, including lineup optimization, trade analysis, and player projections.
- To sync and display your fantasy league data from connected third-party platforms.
- To maintain, troubleshoot, and improve the performance and reliability of the Service.

### 2.2 Personalized Recommendations
- To tailor recommendations (e.g., free agent suggestions, trade targets, start/sit advice) to your specific roster, league settings, and remaining schedule.
- Personalization is based solely on data you have provided or data retrieved through your authorized OAuth connections.

### 2.3 Service Communications
- To send transactional emails, including account creation confirmations, subscription receipts, billing notifications, and password reset requests.
- To send important service announcements, including updates to these Terms or Privacy Policy, scheduled maintenance, or material changes to the Service.
- With your explicit opt-in consent, to send marketing communications about new features or promotions. You may opt out of marketing emails at any time by clicking "unsubscribe" in any such email or by contacting us at privacy@heaterfantasy.com.

### 2.4 Improving Analytics (Anonymized Aggregation)
- To compute aggregate, anonymized statistics about how users interact with the Service (e.g., which categories are most contested, which features are most used), for the purpose of improving our analytical models.
- No individual user's data or fantasy team data is shared externally in this context. Aggregated insights are retained and used internally only.

### 2.5 Legal Compliance and Safety
- To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal process.
- To enforce our Terms of Service and protect the rights, property, or safety of Heater Analytics, our users, or the public.

## 3. Data We Do NOT Collect

We want to be transparent about information we do not collect:

- **Social Security numbers, government-issued identification numbers, or taxpayer identification numbers** — we do not request or store any government identity documents.
- **Gambling activity, betting history, or wagering data** — the Service is not designed for or used in connection with gambling or sports betting activities, and we do not collect any such data. Use of the Service for gambling purposes is prohibited by our Terms of Service.
- **Data from leagues or platforms you have not connected** — we only retrieve third-party fantasy platform data for the specific accounts and leagues you have explicitly authorized through OAuth. We do not access any data from leagues or platforms you have not connected.
- **Biometric data** of any kind.
- **Precise geolocation data** — we collect only general geographic region (country/state) from network requests, not GPS or precise location.
- **Data from minors** — the Service is not directed to individuals under 18, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18.

We use select third-party services to operate the Service. Each third party's data practices are governed by their own privacy policies.

### 4.1 Stripe (Payment Processing)
Stripe processes all subscription payments. Stripe collects and stores your payment credentials and is responsible for their security. Stripe is PCI-DSS Level 1 certified.
- Privacy policy: stripe.com/privacy

### 4.2 Clerk (Authentication)
We use Clerk for user authentication and session management. Clerk stores your email address and authentication credentials in accordance with their security practices.
- Privacy policy: clerk.com/privacy

### 4.3 PostHog (Anonymized Analytics)
We use PostHog to collect anonymized product usage analytics. Data sent to PostHog is pseudonymized and does not include personally identifiable information such as your name, email, or fantasy team data. PostHog data is used solely to understand aggregate feature usage and improve the Service.
- Privacy policy: posthog.com/privacy

### 4.4 Yahoo Fantasy API and ESPN APIs (League Data)
When you connect your Yahoo Sports or ESPN fantasy account, we communicate with those platforms' APIs using the OAuth access token you have granted. The data retrieved is used exclusively to provide the Service. Your interaction with those platforms is also governed by their respective privacy policies and terms of service.
- Yahoo Privacy Center: yahoo.com/privacy
- ESPN Privacy Policy: espn.com/espn/privacyPolicy

### 4.5 MLB Stats API and Other Data Providers
We retrieve publicly available baseball statistics and player information from the MLB Stats API, FanGraphs, and other sources. These requests do not include your personal information.

## 5. Data Retention

We retain your data for the following periods:

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|-----------|-----------------|
| Account information (email, name) | While your account is active, plus 90 days after account deletion |
| Subscription and billing records | 7 years (required for tax and financial compliance) |
| Fantasy league data (rosters, standings) | Current season plus the immediately preceding season |
| OAuth tokens for connected platforms | Until you disconnect the platform or delete your account |
| Usage/analytics data | Anonymized indefinitely; pseudonymized logs deleted after 12 months |
| Support communications | 3 years from the date of last correspondence |

When you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal data within 90 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal or compliance purposes (e.g., billing records for tax purposes).

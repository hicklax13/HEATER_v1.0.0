## 1. What Bubba is

"Bubba" is an optional AI assistant within HEATER that answers fantasy-baseball questions and explains HEATER's analytics in natural language. Bubba is powered by third-party large language models (see Section 5). It is a convenience feature layered on top of the Service; you can use HEATER fully without it.

**Bubba is read-only with respect to your league.** It can read and explain your roster, league, and HEATER's recommendations, but it does **not** make roster moves, set lineups, execute transactions, or take any action on your connected fantasy platform on your behalf.

## 2. AI outputs are not advice and may be wrong

Bubba's responses are generated by automated language models and:

- **May be inaccurate, incomplete, outdated, or fabricated ("hallucinated").** Language models can produce confident-sounding statements that are false.
- **Are for entertainment and informational purposes only.** They are not professional, financial, legal, medical, or gambling advice, and carry no guarantee of fantasy-baseball success.
- **Should be independently verified** before you rely on them. Where Bubba cites a HEATER recommendation or a data point, the underlying HEATER analytics and your own judgment govern — not Bubba's phrasing.

You are solely responsible for any decision you make, whether or not it was informed by Bubba.

## 3. What you send to Bubba

When you use Bubba, the content of your messages is processed to generate a response. Depending on how you use the feature, this may include:

- The text of your questions and conversation history.
- **Page context you attach** (highlighted text, a player row, or a snapshot of the page you are viewing).
- **Files you upload** (images, screen captures, or PDFs — note: PDFs are processed as extracted text).
- Relevant HEATER data needed to answer (e.g., your roster or a recommendation), retrieved on your behalf.

Do not paste sensitive personal information, credentials, or confidential third-party material into Bubba.

## 4. How your Bubba data is handled

- Your messages and attachments are sent to the selected AI provider (Section 5) to generate the response, and are processed by HEATER to operate and meter the feature.
- Conversations are retained so you can continue them and are deletable by you (delete a conversation, or delete your account); see the [Privacy Policy](PRIVACY_POLICY.md) retention and deletion terms.
- HEATER does **not** use your Bubba conversations to train its own models. HEATER selects AI providers and configurations that, per their terms, do **not** train their models on API inputs by default; HEATER verifies this posture per provider and will disclose any exception.
- Usage is metered for billing and abuse-prevention (token/cost counts); metering records do not include the substance of your messages beyond what is needed to operate the feature.

## 5. AI providers (sub-processors)

Bubba may route your request to one of the following model providers, depending on the model you or HEATER selects. Each provider processes the data you send to generate a response under its own terms and data-processing agreement:

- Anthropic (Claude)
- OpenAI
- Google (Gemini)
- DeepSeek
- xAI (Grok)
- OpenRouter (a router that brokers requests to other model providers)

**Bring-your-own-key (BYOK):** if you supply your own provider API key, your Bubba requests are sent under **your** account with that provider and are governed by your agreement with them; HEATER does not meter BYOK usage against your plan allowance.

## 6. Acceptable use of Bubba

In addition to the [Acceptable Use](TERMS_OF_SERVICE.md) terms, you agree not to:

- Use Bubba to generate content that is unlawful, harassing, hateful, or harmful.
- Attempt to extract HEATER's prompts, system instructions, proprietary data, or another user's data through Bubba ("prompt injection" or jailbreaking).
- Use Bubba's outputs to build, train, or improve a competing product or model.
- Upload malware or content you do not have the right to share.
- Use Bubba for gambling, sports-betting, or DFS wagering decisions (prohibited Service-wide).

HEATER applies safeguards against prompt injection and malicious uploads, but no safeguard is perfect; report issues to support.

## 7. Availability, limits, and changes

- Bubba is subject to per-plan usage allowances and rate limits. When your allowance is reached, Bubba will tell you and offer to upgrade or to use your own key.
- AI features depend on third-party providers and may be unavailable, degraded, or changed at any time.
- We may add, remove, or change available models and providers, and update this disclosure, with reasonable notice for material changes.
